Zero the Hero

You are a hero called Zero, in a tiny open world in the Binary kingdom, full of adventures and free exploration, on a journey to find the sword One, with it you can feel the world again and make sense of things, living in eternal peace.

  • Tiny open world - You are completely free to explore and go anywhere you want without limitations
  • Existential journey - Question the meaning of life, ask questions, look for something, suffer from existential crises
  • Varied gameplay - You can be an explorer, warrior, puzzle solver, sneaky thug, or a sweet-talker
  • All kinds of characters - Meet amazing, captivating, pessimistic and strange people
  • Interconnected systems - Set it on fire and watch an entire forest be destroyed
  • Shoulders of giants - Game is inspired by classics like The Legend of Zelda (1986) and Metal Gear Solid franchise
Developer André N. Darcie
Platform(s) Windows, MacOX, Linux, Android and IOS
Engine Godot
Genre(s) Action-adventure
Mode Single-player
